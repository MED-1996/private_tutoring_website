About Me

Professional Tutor - Math, Physics and Computer Science

My name is Mitchell Daddario, and I'm passionate about helping students achieve their maximum potential in math, physics & computer science! I've been a private tutor for the past 2 years and have personally helped hundreds of students improve their grades & understanding in these subjects. My goal is to empower students to become independent problem solvers.

I'm from Buffalo, NY and graduated from Sweet Home high school in 2015. In high school, I found my love for math and science through taking the AP courses my school offered. I received 5's on the AP Calculus AB, AP Calculus BC, AP Physics C: Mechanics, AP Physics C: Electricity and Magnetism and AP Chemistry exams.

After high school, I attended Lehigh University where I graduated with high honors in Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science. During my Sophomore year I was inducted into the Tau Beta Pi Engineering Honor Society for my academic achievements. I was also a member of the D1 cross country and track teams where I was named to the Patriot League Academic Honor Roll every season. When I wasn't busy with my coursework or running, I would always lend a hand to my fellow classmates who were struggling with their engineering courses. College is where I found my passion for tutoring.

After graduating, I started working as a software engineer. While I loved coding, I also really loved teaching, so outside of work I started tutoring on online platforms such as Varsity Tutors & Paper. For a year I tutored on these platforms helping students & honing my tutoring craft. After 1 year on these platforms I decided to start my own private tutoring business so that I could provide a more custom experience for my students.

Currently, I live in Austin, TX and work as a software engineer at Silicon Labs. My main hobbies include reading, biking, skiing, tennis & travel!
