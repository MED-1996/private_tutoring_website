About Me

Professional Tutor - Math, Physics and Computer Science

My name is Mitchell Daddario, and I'm passionate about helping students achieve their maximum potential in math, physics & computer science! I've been a private tutor for the past 2 years and have personally helped hundreds of students improve their grades & understanding in these subjects. My goal is to empower students to become independent problem solvers.

I'm from Buffalo, NY and graduated from Sweet Home high school in 2015. In high school, I was obsessed with math & physics, so much so that I independently studied for 2 math/physics AP exams my senior year! In addition to my academics I was also an elite high school runner. I ran a 4:16 mile my senior year!

After high school, I attended Lehigh University where I graduated with a B.S. in mechanical engineering and computer science. I really enjoyed college because I was exposed to all the possible routes I could take in engineering. The route I became most interested in was computer science! In college I ran D1 cross country and track. I also spent a summer in Malaysia doing research at UTP.

Since graduating from Lehigh, I've worked as a software engineer at both small & large companies. I also started tutoring students one year after graduating, initially working for tutoring companies such as Varsity Tutors and Paper. One year later I began my own private tutoring practice so that I could provide a more custom tutoring experience for my students.

Currently, I live in Austin, TX and work as a software engineer for Silicon Labs. My main hobbies include reading, coding, biking, skiing, tennis & travel!
